Intravenous Nutrient Therapy (IVNT)

Fast Delivery and Fast Effects

The micronutrients are delivered intravenously, which means you will start to feel the effects during their infusion, which will then continue over the following hours and days. Each vitamin infusion takes between 30-60 minutes. Expect to feel refreshed, re-energised & rejuvenated!

More effective than oral supplements

Intravenous therapy means you get a much higher concentration of nutrients delivered direct to your body’s cells – infact, when administered intravenously, you get more than 90% absorption of vitamins, minerals, anti-oxidants and amino acids, compared to oral supplements, where only 15% of the active nutrients may eventually find their way into your blood stream. Your body starts absorbing and utilising the effects of the vitamins as soon as it enters your bloodstream so effects can be felt much sooner than taking oral supplements.

Restore Your Body’s Nutritional Status

The micro-nutrients used are vitamins, minerals and amino acids. These are classified as ‘essential nutrients’ – aptly named as they are essential for our health. Our body is unable to synthesis these nutrients, therefore it is important to get these from food sources. Unfortunately, due to soil depletion, genetically modified and over processed food, many of us don’t get enough of these essential nutrients and research shows a nutritional deficit in the western diet, even when following an optimum dietary regime. By supplementing our levels of nutrients, under the expert advice of a trained healthcare practitioner, your body can be restored to its peak health and maximum energy performance.
